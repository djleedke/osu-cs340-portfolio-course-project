Overview

Fine Midwestern Dining owns up to 10 high-end restaurants in a city. The company would like to create a recipe repository to share amongst its chefs and management team. The idea is that chefs can use it to look up recipes from each other and gain inspiration to create more. Management can use the database to look at star ratings of dishes and use that data to market appropriately.

The repository would include recipe data about ingredients and combinations of recipes that form dishes. Each recipe is created by a chef, which holds a position of either head chef or sous chef. Each chef works at a single restaurant. We estimate that there will be roughly 40-50 active users w/ approximately 5-10 original recipes per Chef.
